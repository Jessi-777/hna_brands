The Problem & The Opportunity

College athletes
are a brand.
Most just don't know it yet.

The Old Reality

College athletes spend years building a following, a reputation, and a fanbase — and most of that value disappears the moment their career ends. No brand. No income stream. No legacy. Just a highlight reel.

The HNA Opportunity

NIL changed everything. Athletes can now monetize their name, image, and likeness while they're still competing. HNA is the infrastructure to make that real — a full brand-building platform purpose-built for college athletes.

What HNA Solves

Most athletes don't know how to design products, set up an online store, handle orders, or manage fulfillment. HNA handles every single one of those things — athletes just show up and lead.

Why Now

The window to build a brand is during your college career — when you have an active audience, school loyalty, and cultural moment. HNA lets you activate that window before it closes.
